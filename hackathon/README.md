# Agent Academy Live Challenge Contest

![Agent Academy Live Hackathon Banner](./assets/banner.png)

## Build real AI agents, prove what you've learned, win recognition

---

**A developer contest running May 12 – June 2, 2026, kicked off live during [Agent Academy Live](https://aka.ms/agent-academy) on May 12.**

Apply what you've learned from the [Agent Academy](https://github.com/microsoft/agent-academy) curriculum to build real, working AI agents using Microsoft AI tools. Compete for $12,000 in prizes, earn recognition, and help shape the future of the Agent Academy curriculum.

📜 **[Official Contest Rules](./OFFICIAL_RULES.md)** — Full legal terms and conditions

⚠️ **Important**: Before submitting, read our [Disclaimer](./DISCLAIMER.md) — do not upload confidential information.

> 📚 Looking for the Agent Academy curriculum? Visit the [main README](../README.md).

## Contest Overview

A connected program that validates the Agent Academy curriculum by converting learning into real, working AI agents:

1. **📚 Learn** — Engage with [Agent Academy](https://aka.ms/agent-academy) curriculum modules
1. **📺 Live** — Agent Academy Live delivers structured instruction, demos, and inspiration
1. **🔨 Build** — Apply what you learned to build real agents
1. **📤 Share** — Submit demos and architectures for review
1. **🔄 Improve** — Your feedback and insights inform curriculum and product evolution

**Open to everyone** — all skill levels welcome! Choose a participation track that matches your experience.

## Entry Period

The Contest starts at 12:00 a.m. Pacific Time (PT) on **May 12, 2026**, and ends at 11:59 p.m. PT on **June 2, 2026**.

## How to Enter

### 1. Register

Register at the official Agent Academy registration page: **[https://aka.ms/agent-academy-live](https://aka.ms/agent-academy-live)**

### 2. Build Your Agent

Follow the instructions and build an agent that meets **all** of the following criteria:

- **Curriculum-aligned**: Must demonstrate one or more Agent Academy modules, patterns, or concepts that align with the Agent Academy curriculum for the applicable Track.
- **Problem-solving focused**: Each agent must address a real problem with a defined target user.
- **Architected systems**: Submissions require an architecture overview with components, data flow, and integration points.
- **Microsoft Ecosystem**: Each agent must use at least one (1) of the following Microsoft products and services:
  - Microsoft Copilot Studio
  - Azure OpenAI Service
  - Microsoft Graph API
  - Power Automate

### 3. Create a Demo Video

Make a demo video featuring your agent in action. Your video may be **no longer than five (5) minutes** in length.

### 4. Submit Your Entry

Submit your agent and demo video via GitHub issues at: **[https://aka.ms/agentacademy/submit](https://aka.ms/agentacademy/submit)**

Pick one of the following Tracks:

| Track | Details |
|-------|---------|
| 🟢 **Recruit** | [https://microsoft.github.io/agent-academy/recruit/](https://microsoft.github.io/agent-academy/recruit/) |
| 🟡 **Operative** | [https://microsoft.github.io/agent-academy/operative/](https://microsoft.github.io/agent-academy/operative/) |
| 🔴 **Special Ops** | [https://microsoft.github.io/agent-academy/special-ops/](https://microsoft.github.io/agent-academy/special-ops/) |
| 🤝 **Cowork Collective** | [https://microsoft.github.io/agent-academy/cowork-collective/](https://microsoft.github.io/agent-academy/cowork-collective/) |

### Entry Limits

- **One (1) entry per Track** and **two (2) entries overall** during the Entry Period.
- Any attempt to obtain more than the stated number of entries by using multiple accounts, email addresses, identities, or other methods will void your entries and you may be disqualified.
- Use of any automated system to participate is prohibited.

## Eligible Entry Requirements

To be eligible, an entry must meet:

- Your entry must be your own original work
- The demo video must be solely your own work (filming, editing, graphic design, etc.)
- Your entry cannot have been selected as a winner in any other contest
- You must have obtained all consents, approvals, or licenses required for submission
- Your entry may NOT contain content that is obscene, offensive, violent, defamatory, disparaging, illegal, or that promotes alcohol, illegal drugs, tobacco, or a particular political agenda

## Judging Criteria

All submissions are scored by a panel of judges based on the following criteria:

| Criteria | Weight |
|----------|--------|
| **Accuracy & Relevance** — Meets challenge requirements | 25% |
| **Technical Execution** — Clear problem-solving approach | 25% |
| **Creativity & Originality** — Novel ideas or unexpected execution | 15% |
| **User Experience & Presentation** — Clear, polished, demoable | 15% |
| **Reliability & Safety** — Solid patterns, avoids obvious pitfalls | 10% |
| **Use Case Impact** — Most impactful use case | 10% |

Winners will be selected within **fourteen (14) days** following the Entry Period from among all eligible entries received.

In the event of a tie, an additional judge will break the tie based on the criteria above. The decisions of the judges are final and binding.
